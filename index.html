About Lubabalo Dlwathi

My name is Lubabalo Dlwathi, and I am passionate about serving the student community at Wits University. As a candidate for SRC President, I am committed to bringing positive change to our campus by amplifying the voices of students and ensuring everyone is heard.

With my experience in leadership roles, I have a clear vision for making student life better by focusing on inclusivity, transparency, and development. Together, we can build a future where everyone thrives.

My Campaign Goals

  • Tackle food insecurity by advocating for partnerships with local businesses to support our food bank and outreach programs.
  • Address accommodation issues by improving support systems and resources for securing suitable housing.
  • Enhance mental health support by increasing access to counseling services, promoting peer support networks, and reducing stigma through workshops.
  • Create on-campus employment opportunities to support students financially and provide valuable work experience.
  • Advocate for increased financial support and resources to combat financial exclusion and ensure no student is left behind.
